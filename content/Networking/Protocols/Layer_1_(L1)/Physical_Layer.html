Physical Layer

The physical layer represents ethernet cable, fiber optics, bluetooth signal sender modules . All these physically transmit data.The data in these devices has only 2 forms 1's and 0's . The format (protocol) of the data comes from the sender and is reassembled by the receiver. The data signal could be electricityin case of ethernet, light in case of fiber optics, radio waves

Layer 4

Also known as Transport Layer

Deals with the actual service that is handling the traffic. The service that is handling the traffic has to listen on the port that is mentioned in the packet. So this layer depends on the port .

Protocols used are TCP , UDP mainly, there are other protocols that operate in this layer
